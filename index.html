FAQ

How do I calculate load (Watt) for UPS/IPS?
Add the watt of each appliance you will run (fan, light, router, PC, etc.). Then we select UPS/IPS capacity and battery based on runtime.
Which is better: Online UPS or Offline UPS?
Online UPS is best for sensitive equipment (server/IT/medical) because it provides cleaner, continuous power. Offline UPS is cheaper for general use.
What solar system should I choose: on-grid, off-grid or hybrid?
On-grid reduces electricity bill but no backup. Off-grid gives backup but needs battery. Hybrid gives both benefits depending on design and budget.
